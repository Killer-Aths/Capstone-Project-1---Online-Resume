A REVIEW ON IMAGE COMPRESSION TECHNIQUES

This paper presents an extensive review of various image types and the diverse techniques utilized in image compression. Our thorough examination culminates in the proposal of a generalized method for image compression, focusing on reducing image size while preserving image quality.Image compression plays a pivotal role in facilitating the efficient transmission and storage of vast amounts of digital image data. Its applications span across diverse domains such as television broadcasting, satellite-based remote sensing, and long-distance communication. Simultaneously, effective image storage is crucial for preserving critical data including medical images, satellite imagery, documents, and personal photographs.

In this study, we delve into different image types and explore a range of compression techniques to address the multifaceted challenges associated with the transmission and storage of digital images. Notably, we demonstrate two fundamental approaches: lossless compression technique employing Huffman coding, and lossy compression technique employing Block Truncation Coding. Through our investigation, we aim to contribute to the advancement of image compression methodologies, paving the way for more efficient utilization of digital resources in various fields.
